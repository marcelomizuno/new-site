# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Business owners in high-value service industries — dentists, lawyers, clinic operators, architects. They arrive from ads, referrals, or organic search. Context: they're busy running their practice, losing leads to slow response times, and looking for a solution that works without adding to their workload.

**Job to be done**: Stop losing revenue to unanswered leads. Install a system that captures, qualifies, and converts every lead — 24/7, automatically — so they can focus on their craft.

## Product Purpose

Revenue infrastructure for service businesses. We install marketing systems (WhatsApp bots, Instagram automation, CRM dashboards, landing pages, follow-up sequences) that capture, qualify, and convert every lead — 24/7, automatically.

**Success looks like**: Every lead gets a response in under 1 minute. Bookings increase. Revenue per channel is visible. The system runs without daily oversight.

## Brand Personality

**Bold & Technical** — confident, data-driven, no-nonsense.

We sell results, not tools. Every metric is real. Every claim is backed by data. We don't hedge or qualify — we state what we do and prove it with numbers (150+ businesses served, 87% leads recovered, <1min response time).

Voice: direct, specific, zero fluff. "We install revenue machines" not "We help businesses grow."

## Anti-references

**Generic SaaS look** — the cream/sand/beige body bg, the identical card grids, the tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows above every section, the hero-metric template. The AI-generated aesthetic that makes every startup look interchangeable.

Also avoid:
- Overly corporate or stiff design (too much whitespace, muted everything)
- Playful or whimsical touches that undermine credibility
- Gradient text, glassmorphism, hand-drawn illustrations
- Side-stripe borders on cards

## Design Principles

1. **Revenue-First** — Every element exists to drive conversions. If it doesn't move a visitor toward "Get Your Free Diagnosis," it's decoration.

2. **Show, Don't Tell** — Metrics beat adjectives. "87% leads recovered" beats "industry-leading." Real numbers, real proof.

3. **Speed as Product** — The site itself must be fast (static HTML, no heavy JS). Response time under 1 minute is the core promise — the design should feel that fast.

4. **Expert Confidence** — We serve operators who make high-stakes decisions (dental implants, legal cases, surgeries). The design must project authority, not trendy aesthetics.

5. **Global Reach, Local Feel** — Multi-language (EN, PT, ES) with WhatsApp-first communication. The design should feel native to each market, not translated.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

General best practices (WCAG AA standard):
- Minimum 4.5:1 contrast for body text, 3:1 for large text
- Visible focus states on all interactive elements
- Semantic HTML with proper ARIA labels
- Reduced motion alternatives for animations
- Touch targets ≥44px on mobile
